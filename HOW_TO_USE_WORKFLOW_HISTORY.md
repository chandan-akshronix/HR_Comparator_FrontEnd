## Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the history dropdown"

**Reason**: No completed workflows yet  
**Solution**: Complete at least one workflow to 100%

### "My history disappeared"

**Possible Causes**:
- Browser data was cleared
- Using different browser/device
- LocalStorage was disabled

**Solution**: Complete new workflows to rebuild history

### "Can't switch back to current"

**Check**:
1. Is "Back to Current" button visible?
2. Try clicking the dropdown again
3. Select "Current Workflow (Live)"
